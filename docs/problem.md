# Problem
- Tanzanian businesses depend on foreign and global social media platforms to advertise their businesses.
- Although it's free, they face dangers of being blocked out of their accounts, or aggressive competition when they share a direct social media link or a username to a client.
- Some business do not have any social media presence at all, so they are unable to provide online access of their business to their client.

# Target Customers
- The first version of spp targets medium-size shops, that already have online presence. Eg (Instagram, facebook, etc)
- It also targets SMEs that do not have a domain name yet eg (business.co.tz) 
- They already have a brand, eg name, etc
- They have a physical shop, with their shop listed on google maps

# Current Situation
- They largely depend on social media, but want to start building a brand
- They don't have a domain name.
- They desire more physical visits to their shop.
- They are not ready to pay big bucks for a full website.

# Proposed Solution
- One simple landing page for each business
- Call to action buttons included (Call, Text, WhatsApp, Visit)
- Direct SMS and WhatsApp message buttons, that sends to text/whatsapp when hit sends with prefilled text in the chatbox)
- Cheap updates, pay once to do link changes, color theme change etc. 
- Direct map location and direction to the shop location.
- Pay once per year, cheap price for the landing page.
- Get visit analytics reports, weekly, through local sms, increase or decrease in total page visits, and total button clicks.
- Mutlillingual, preset per business, English/Swahili and Auto theme, light or dark depending on device settings.
- Domain name for each business to protect their online presence, and later when they have a website or need branded emails, they can use the same domain

# Why it matters in Tanzania
- Tanzanians use phones more than computers.
- WhatsApp and SMS is more common locally
- Websites are expensive to build and to maintain.
- Custom domain in an instagram bio can lead to client visiting facebook. So it increases brand exposure.
- Can help customers visit local shops, and trust the shop.

# Version 0.1 Success Criteria
- One business page loads correctly
- Business information is displayed correctly.
- WhatsApp, SMS and call buttons work correctly.
- Page is mobile responsive.
- One real business can use it. 
- English/Swahili switcher works perfectly.
- Social links display correctly.
